# The Infinite Layered Web (ILW)

**A Structured Riemannian Manifold Extension of Quantum Mechanics with Residual Inter-Branch Dynamics, Gravity Coupling, and Hybrid String-Inspired Features**

arXiv preprint (coming soon) • Paper PDF: [ilw_paper.pdf](paper/ilw_paper.pdf)

## What is ILW?

ILW adds one geometric postulate to quantum mechanics: the space of decohered pointer states forms a Riemannian manifold with a hybrid Bures + configuration-space metric. This produces:
- Distance-dependent residual couplings that survive decoherence
- Geometric origin of the Born rule
- Curved Schrödinger equation on the manifold
- Semiclassical gravity sourced by web probability density
- Hybrid string-inspired compact dimensions

## Simulations

All figures in the paper are generated by `src/ilw_simulations.py`.

**Key results included:**
- 1D ring, 2D/3D gravity-coupled patches
- 10×10×6 hybrid string-inspired grid with full decoherence
- Born-rule deviation scans, quantum chaos spectra, continuum limits
